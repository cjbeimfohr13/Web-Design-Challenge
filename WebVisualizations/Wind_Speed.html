Wind Speed

While not definitively proven, it is aparent that there is a minor correlation between the windspeed and the city latitude. Although not a large difference and outliers still need to be determined, it looks like there are larger gusts of wind further north rather than south. I find that extremely interesting because of the horrific storms and high wind speeds that occur in the tropical storm zones. The higher wind speeds up north could be due to the type of wind that went through that day or could have to do with the antartic. However, I have heard that NZ has the strongest wind in the world rendering my hypothesis incorrect.
